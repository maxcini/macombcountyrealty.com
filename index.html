FAQ: Selling Options

Fast & Direct

All-Cash Offer FAQs

Best for speed, certainty, and selling completely "as-is" without listing stress.

Will you make a lowball offer on my home?

No. Our cash offers are calculated transparently based on recent neighborhood sales data and projected repair costs. While cash offers sit below top market retail value, you eliminate agent commissions, closing fees, staging costs, and months of holding expenses—often netting a similar amount out-of-pocket with zero hassle.

Are there any hidden fees or extra costs?

Absolutely none. What we offer is exactly what you walk away with. We cover 100% of the closing costs, and because there are no real estate agents involved, you pay zero commissions, marketing fees, or surprise transactional costs.

Do I need to clean up or make repairs first?

Not a single thing. We buy houses in absolute "as-is" condition. You can leave behind unwanted furniture, trash, dirt, or costly structural issues—our team handles everything after the purchase is finalized.

Maximum Value

Open Market Listing FAQs

Best for homeowners looking to capture full, top-dollar retail value.

Why list on the market if a cash offer is faster?

If your house is already updated or you aren't under a strict timeline, listing on the open market exposes your property to traditional, emotional retail buyers. This maximum exposure drives competition, helping you capture the highest potential sale price.

How long does a traditional market listing take?

The timeline depends entirely on local buyer demand, pricing strategy, and your home's condition. While a direct cash sale can wrap up in just a few days, an open market listing usually averages between 30 to 90 days from the initial launch to the closing.

How do you determine the listing price?

We analyze recent sales of similar homes, current competition, local market conditions, and your property's unique features to recommend a pricing strategy. The right price helps attract qualified buyers and maximize your chances of receiving the strongest possible offers.
